About Olivia

Hello there!


I'm Olivia Petrillo and currently I'm a freshman at Northeastern University in Boston, MA. I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Biology.

In high school I wasn't exposed to much computer science, aside from an intro course or two. This field attracted me my senior year, with the challenge and problem solving aspects of computer science reeling me in. I love having the opportunity at Northeastern to delve deeper into two of my interests: math and biology, and flourish as an individual.

Outside of school, I enjoy indulging in game shows, books (Currently reading Stephen King's IT!), and relaxing by the water.
